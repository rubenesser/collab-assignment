Rissani, Morocco

Rissani is a beautiful hub for all the region's commerce. Situated near Erfoud, Rissani sees a daily soukh (market) with a real, authentic feel.

The Soukh

Rissani is a tiny town in the countryside, surrounded by tiny little villages. It's the biggest settlement for a while, and it houses a massive soukh that contains in it the wares of all the tradesmen and farmers for kilometres around.

The raw authenticity of the soukh is a rare sight, especially after visiting Marrakech, which is more for tourists. Cow's heads are sold at the butcher stall next to a wonderfully full room of dates (in the right season), next to cabinet makers selling furniture.

The soukh is central to the town, but already tour busses from nearby hotels pass through there, signaling that the undiscovered realism of the place may soon change.
